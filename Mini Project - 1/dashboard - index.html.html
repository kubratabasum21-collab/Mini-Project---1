Student Time Management Dashboard

Dashboard | Tasks | Timetable | Goals | Progress | Profile

This dashboard helps students plan their time effectively, manage tasks, set goals, and track academic progress and It provides a single platform where students can view their daily tasks,study time table and personal profile information Overall, a student dashboard plays an important role in developing good time management and self-discipline among students.

Through regular monitoring of academic performance, students can identify their strengths and areas that need improvement. A student dashboard promotes self-discipline, accountability, and motivation, which are essential qualities for academic success. It also supports independent learning by helping students take responsibility for their own progress. In addition, the dashboard improves focus by providing a structured environment that reduces distractions.
